    Attack on Titan

    Copied

    Shingeki no Kyojin

    Synopsis

    Set in a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction, walls protect the last remnants of human civilization from gigantic humanoid creatures known as Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends as they join the military to fight the Titans and uncover dark secrets about their world.

    Main Characters

    • Eren Yeager

      Determined and often reckless, driven by a desire for freedom and vengeance against the Titans.

    • Mikasa Ackerman

      A highly skilled soldier and Eren's adoptive sister, fiercely protective and composed.

    • Armin Arlert

      Strategic thinker whose intelligence often turns the tide of battles.
