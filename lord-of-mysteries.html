    Synopsis

    In a world where steam technology and mysticism coexist, a young man named Klein Moretti finds himself embroiled in a web of intrigue and danger after accidentally acquiring a mysterious artifact. As he navigates this treacherous new world, Klein must confront powerful foes, uncover hidden truths, and ultimately decide the fate of his own existence. Later, creating his own club - "TAROT"

    Main Characters

    • Klein Moretti

      A former history professor turned detective, who becomes entangled in the world of mysteries and supernatural occurrences. Also known as leader of the Tarot club - "The Fool"
